About Jill
"My passion is educating the patient. My job satisfaction is relieving their pain."

Jill McLaggan is a highly experienced chronic pain specialist based in Edinburgh, Scotland. With over 15 years of clinical practice, Jill combines evidence-based physiotherapy with holistic approaches including Craniosacral Therapy and Dry Needling to address the root causes of persistent pain.

Her philosophy is built on deep patient education and collaborative care — empowering individuals to understand their body and take control of their healing journey. Whether you suffer from back pain, neck pain, or complex chronic conditions, Jill's approach is always calm, thorough, and deeply personal.
